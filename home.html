Principal's message





Life is a journey! The path we take, what we look back on, and what we look forward to is up to us. We determine our destination. This Journey of excellence in perennial. For us at Greenfield Convent School Habra the Journey has just started. The odyssey has just begun, with the vision of providing holistic education to the young citizens of India. It is a cornerstone in helping generations of students to dream visualize and plan for their future. We are committed to equip our students with human values, culture and heritage along with academic rigors.

“Our dedicated and devoted faculty imbibes among our children the secret of hard work and the importance of ethics. They Provide quality education and motivates each student to stretch and reach greater heights. A proactive teaching methodology ensure the participation of Every child in the class.

A child goes to School not just gain Knowledge or information but to learn the essential threads of group dynamics.

Adjustment developing interpersonal skills to line in a connected world. Our school provides a plethora of platforms through scholastic, co-scholastic activities, values through life skill education, personality development, sportsmanship spirit and team abilities, which help any child to evolve as a responsible citizen. Our beautiful campus with cognitively rich atmosphere helps the children to grow in a proper learning environment. One realizes that family values dedication, purity and bio-conservation are the maxims that we live by” we are prepared to create our own Masterpieces on the canvas of education with brushes of devotion, faith and love. The entire staffs of GCS, Habra moves with a mission of providing its children with a mission of providing its children with all facilities to make them good and successful human beings. We want each one of our students to grow the wings of ambition and soar, soar like a falcon towards the sky. With their feet firmly planted in core Indian Values to achievements is deeply embedded in the heart of our students as they strive ahead to win accolades.
