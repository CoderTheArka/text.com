ADMMISION

Documents Required for Admission

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Class Report Card (For Admission to Class UKG to Class VIII)
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Immunization Card
  • 4 Photographs (Passport Size) of the Student.
  • Aadhar Card.

General Instructions

1. The requisite documents mentioned above along with a photocopy of the fee Slip must be submitted within the stipulated date for securing admission.

2. It is mandatory for all students from Class I onwards to submit the Transfer Certificate and Report Card/Mark Sheet of the last school attended by them. No student will be admitted to school without submission of TC from the previous institution. The Student will not be placed in a class higher than that for which the Transfer Certificate qualifies him/her.

3. The managing Council of the school reserves the right to have the final decision in all matters related to the admission.

FEE & BILLING

1. In case of withdrawal, fee once paid at the time of admission is not refundable.

2. Session fees, Education fees and Transport maintenance charges will be subject to annual revision.
