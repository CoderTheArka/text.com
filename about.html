About School

Greenfield Convent School, Habra is an English medium Co-educational School following CBSE curriculum. The Mission of Greenfield is to provide exceptional education combining the traditional Indian values with a truly modern approach adhering to the international standard. Enabling students to be knowledgeable, kind, committed, global minded and confident.

WHY GREENFIELD CONVENT SCHOOL ?

Skill Enhancement Programme

GCS has set up a counselling cell by well trained counselor and remedial teachers. We work towards identifying and rectifying learning difficulties and behavioural problems.

Digital learning through Interactive software

We have moved from blackboard teaching to Interactive Smart Board teaching, making the teaching-learning process not only enjoyable but innovative, leading to student interaction, interest and curiosity

Thoughts

In our constant endeavour and search to provide our students with better learning & development Goals, we are initiating ‘Thoughts Program’ for our students. It is a unique concept for the development of Higher Education Thinking Skills & Thinking Processes for Children.

Think Lab

When one thinks of science, one only thinks of it as experiments and observations, however one needs to think beyond test tubes and burners. It is a subject that helps one to be organized and think logically. Think lab is a place which incubates inquisitiveness, prerequisite to be a successful student of science. ‘Think Lab’ provides each student a science kit of his own. Through this kit a student learns about the common apparatus used in science.
